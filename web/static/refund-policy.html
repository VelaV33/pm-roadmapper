1. How to cancel

You can cancel your Roadmap OS subscription at any time using any of these methods:

  • Email us at velasabelo.com@gmail.com with the subject line "Cancel subscription" and the email address associated with your account. We will process the cancellation within 1 business day.
  • Paystack dashboard — if you have a Paystack account, you can manage your subscription directly from your Paystack payment history.

No cancellation fee applies. You do not need to give a reason, although we appreciate feedback so we can improve the Service.

2. What happens when you cancel

What What happens
Paid features Remain active until the end of your current billing period. You are not charged again.
Your data Preserved indefinitely. Your account reverts to the free Basic tier. All your roadmaps, plans, OKRs, templates, and other content remain accessible.
Team members Shared roadmaps and team assignments remain in place. Collaborators can still view shared content.
AI features Disabled at the end of the billing period (AI features require the Pro tier). Your AI API keys remain stored locally on your device.
Resubscribing You can resubscribe at any time from pmroadmapper.com/pricing. Your data and settings will still be there.
We never delete your data because you cancelled. Your roadmaps are yours. Cancellation only affects access to paid features. If you want your data deleted entirely, see our Privacy Policy (Section 7 — Your Rights).

3. Refund eligibility

3.1 Satisfaction guarantee — first 14 days

If you are a first-time subscriber and are unsatisfied with Roadmap OS for any reason within the first 14 days of your initial paid subscription, you are entitled to a full refund — no questions asked. Simply email us within the 14-day window.

3.2 After 14 days

After the 14-day satisfaction window, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. We will typically issue a refund if:

  • Service outage — the Service was materially unavailable for an extended period (more than 48 consecutive hours) during your billing cycle, and the outage was within our control.
  • Billing error — you were charged incorrectly (duplicate charge, wrong amount, charged after cancellation).
  • Feature misrepresentation — a feature advertised on our marketing site or pricing page does not function as described, and we are unable to resolve the issue within 7 business days of your report.

3.3 Situations where refunds are not issued

  • Change of mind after 14 days — once the satisfaction window has passed, we do not issue refunds for change of mind. You can cancel at any time to prevent future charges.
  • Partial months — we do not pro-rate refunds for unused days within a billing period. When you cancel, you retain access until the end of the period you have already paid for.
  • Third-party costs — charges from your AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) are between you and that provider. We have no visibility into or control over those costs.
  • Account suspension for ToS violation — if your account was suspended for violating our Terms of Service, no refund is issued.

4. How to request a refund

Email velasabelo.com@gmail.com with:

  1. Subject line: "Refund request"
  2. The email address associated with your Roadmap OS account
  3. A brief description of why you are requesting a refund
  4. The approximate date of the charge in question

We aim to acknowledge your request within 2 business days and provide a resolution within 5 business days.

5. Refund processing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. We cannot refund to a different card or bank account.

6. Disputes and escalation

If you are unhappy with the outcome of a refund request, or have any other billing dispute, we follow a three-step resolution process:

Step 1 — Direct resolution (first contact)

Email velasabelo.com@gmail.com describing the issue. Most disputes are resolved at this stage within 5 business days. We approach every case with the goal of finding a fair outcome.

Step 2 — Escalation review

If you are not satisfied with the initial response, reply to the same email thread with "Escalation request" in the subject line. A senior team member will review the case independently and respond within 7 business days with a final decision.

Step 3 — External resolution

If the dispute remains unresolved after Steps 1 and 2, you may:

  • File a complaint with the National Consumer Commission of South Africa at thencc.gov.za.
  • Contact your card issuer or bank to initiate a chargeback (see Section 7).
  • If you are in the EU/EEA, you may use the Online Dispute Resolution platform at ec.europa.eu/odr.

We prefer to resolve disputes directly and will engage constructively with any external resolution process.

7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback (payment reversal) through your bank or card issuer, your subscription will be immediately suspended pending resolution. We encourage you to contact us directly first (see Section 6) — chargeback proceedings are slower and more adversarial for both parties.

If a chargeback is filed for a charge that we believe was legitimate, we will submit evidence to the card network. If the chargeback is upheld, we will honour the reversal and your account will be downgraded to the free Basic tier.

8. Special circumstances

  • Service discontinuation — if we ever discontinue the Service entirely, all active subscribers will receive a full refund for any unused portion of their current billing period, plus at least 30 days' notice and an opportunity to export their data.
  • Extended outage — if the Service experiences a significant outage (more than 48 hours) affecting your ability to use paid features, we will proactively offer billing credits or partial refunds to affected subscribers.
  • Hardship — if you are experiencing financial hardship and need to cancel but are concerned about losing access, contact us. We will work with you to find a solution — we would rather keep you on a free tier than lose you as a user.
